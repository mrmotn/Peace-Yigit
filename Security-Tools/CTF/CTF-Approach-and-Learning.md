## ðŸ§­ My CTF Methodology

I approach CTFs with a calm, methodical mindset.

### 1. Understand the Problem
- Read the description carefully
- Identify what is given and what is missing
- Avoid assumptions

### 2. Map the Surface
- Identify inputs and outputs
- Observe behavior changes
- Look for patterns

### 3. Research Before Acting
- Check documentation
- Read similar write-ups after attempting
- Understand the underlying concept

### 4. Test Hypotheses
- Try ideas incrementally
- Observe results
- Adjust approach logically

### 5. Extract the Lesson
- Why did this work?
- What was the intended vulnerability?
- How could this be prevented?
